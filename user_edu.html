User Education

  • Interested in Using CAST
    What is FusiformCAST?

    FusiformCAST is a software platform that enables clinicians and fabricators to communicate instantly and effectively throughout the O&P ordering and manufacturing process. Using several features of the platform, clinicians can fill out, edit, and submit orders digitally, store patient data securely, and message fabricators instantly.





    Frequently Asked Questions
    Is FusiformCAST HIPAA compliant?

    Yes, all data from your CAST account is encrypted and then stored in a HIPAA compliant, secure vault in the cloud. Neither fabricators nor Fusiform have access to any of that data, only you and the rest of your clinic do.

    How much does it cost to use FusiformCAST?

    FusiformCAST is FREE for clinics!

  • Exploring the Dashboard
    1. What is the dashboard?

    Each user within your organization’s CAST account has their own dashboard, which features the patients and orders that that clinician is responsible for or keeping track of.

    2. How do I add patients/orders to my dashboard?

    In order to see patients and orders on your dashboard, you have to be “following” those patients and orders. Find your patient or order in the “All Patients” and “All Orders” tabs respectively, and click either the “Patient Actions” or “Order Actions” tab on the right. Then, simply click “follow” on the drop down menu, and it should appear on your dashboard.

    3. How do I search for a specific patient?

    Both your dashboard and the “All Patients” tab have search bars where you can type in a specific patient’s name.





    Frequently Asked Questions
    Why aren’t there any patients or orders listed on my dashboard?

    In order to see patients and orders on your dashboard, you have to be “following” those patients and orders. Find your patient or order in the “All Patients” and “All Orders” tabs respectively, and click either the “Patient Actions” or “Order Actions” tab on the right. Then, simply click follow on the drop down menu, and it should appear on your dashboard.

    One of my patients is missing from my dashboard. How do I add them to the dashboard?

    Go to the “All Patients” tab on the left-hand side of the page, and search for your patient using the search bar at the top. Once you’ve clicked on that patient, click the “Patient Actions” tab on the right, and select “Follow patient” from the drop down menu. Your patient should now be on your dashboard.


  • Adding Patient + Media
    1. How do I add patient to the platform?

    To add a new patient, first click the “Add Patient” tab on the left side of the screen. Then, simply fill out the new patient form and click “Save Patient.” Your new patient will now appear on your dashboard!

    2. How do I add media to my patient’s account?

    To add media to your patient’s account, first go to your dashboard and click on the relevant patient. Then, click on the blue “Add Media” tab on the top right. Either drag and drop a media file into the box, or click to select a specific file from your computer. You can upload photos, videos, and even 3D scans of your patient.

    Once the file has been uploaded, you should see a green check mark next to the file. Now, go back to the patient overview by clicking the “Overview” tab on the top right. You should see your new media file after clicking the “Media” tab under your patient’s name.

    3. How do I view and annotate a media file?

    Once you are on a patient overview page, click the “Media” tab below the patient information fields. To view a media file, simply click on the file name and use your mouse and the slider to zoom in/out or rotate (if the file is a 3D scan). To add an annotation to your file, scroll down the page and click on the “Create” tab within the Annotations box. After you have adjusted the image/scan, click “Lock and Add Annotations” and then click on the area of the image you wish to annotate.

  • Ordering Devices
    1. How do I start ordering a device for my patient?

    Once you are on a patient overview page, click on the blue “Add Order” tab on the top right of the screen. On the order page, there will be a list of devices currently available on your account. To search for a specific vendor or device, simply type in the search bar above the device list. Once you’ve found the device you want, select it from the list and click “Save & Continue” at the bottom of the page. If you don’t see a device that you need, reach out to us at support@fusiform.co and we’ll work on adding that order form to your account.

    2. How do I complete my order form?

    To complete an order form, click on the “Edit Order” tab on the top right of the page and select “Medical Information” from the drop down menu and fill out all required fields. Once you’ve completed the order form, go back to the order overview page by clicking on the “Overview” tab.

    3. How do I add media files to my order form?

    On the Order Overview page, click on the “Attach Media” tab on the top right of the screen. Once you’ve selected the correct media file, click the “Attach” button on the left and then “Save & Continue” on the bottom. Go back to the Order Overview page by clicking the “Overview” tab on the top right. Your newly attached media file should now appear under “Attached Media.”

    4. How do I send my order form?

    If you are satisfied with your order and have attached all relevant media files to your order, click on the gray “Order Actions” tab on the right side of the screen. If the fabricator you’re ordering from is on FusiformCAST, you can click “Send to Vendor,” which will send a completed, digital form straight to the fabricator. Otherwise, click “Generate Report,” which will automatically download a PDF of your order form, which you can print or email to the fabricator.

  • Communicating with Vendors/Clinicians
    How do I communicate with a vendor?

    Once you’ve started on order for a patient, you can communicate with the relevant vendor using the Comments box on the Order Overview page. If the vendor is on FusiformCAST, you can simply type a message into the Comments box and they’ll get an instant message on their side of the platform. Our messaging system is fast, efficient, and completely HIPAA compliant.
