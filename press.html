Fusiform joins NY-based Human Condition Labs
May 18, 2016

Exponential Manufacturing convened a select group of 400 leading executives, investors, and entrepreneurs from around the globe in Boston to explore how exponential technologies are changing the way humanity designs, makes, produces, moves, sells, and consumes the material world. The Exponential Conference Series is held by Singularity University, a benefit corporation that provides educational programs, innovative partnerships and a startup accelerator to help individuals, businesses, institutions, investors, NGOs and governments understand cutting-edge technologies, and how to utilize these technologies to positively impact billions of people.

Fusiform was invited, in conjunction with Human Condition Labs, to present at the Exponential Manufacturing Innovation Lab.
