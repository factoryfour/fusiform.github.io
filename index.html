Vision

7.3 million people will use orthotic devices by 2020. People still wait nearly a month to receive a device only to trash it after 6 months.

Orthotics should give people mobility and confidence, not slow them down. So we're changing it up.

Our technology will get you an orthotic in hours, not weeks, making sure you have your best foot forward sooner than later.

Getting everybody on the same page gets the job done faster and better. Introducing CAST, a versatile and powerful digital platform to make custom fabrication efficient and hassle-free.

  • Time-Efficient: Spend 10 minutes scanning a patient instead of an hour casting with plaster.
  • Collaborative: Clinics and central fabricators view and annotate the 3D models together, live-messaging and collaborating to get the perfect device, every time.
  • Completely Digital Ordering: Clinics simply upload a standard STL/OBJ file, choose their favorite central fabricator and type out a custom digital form for the device they want.
  • Patient & Order Tracking: Never keep a customer waiting. View status updates on every patient and order to make sure you are getting the device to a patient on time.
