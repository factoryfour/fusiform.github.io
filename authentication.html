Signing Up for FusiformCAST

Creating your CAST account and logging in with Two-Factor Authentication.

  • Clinic Sign Up
    Thank you for your interest in using CAST! This sign up method is for clinics and clinic systems. If you are a vendor then please contact support@fusiform.co and we will work with you to set up an account.
    1. Go to FusiformCAST's Clinic Application

    Click on this link to go to the clinic application for CAST.

    2. Sign Up and Confirm Email

    Click the Sign Up tab and fill out the necessary information on the form. Hit Submit and a confirmation email will be sent to your specified email. Confirm from that email and your account will be created and you will be able to login.

    Sign Up Tab on clinic.fusiform.co


    Fill Out Sign Up Form


    Verify your Email

    3. Add Users to Your System

    Only the administrator of a specific location or team should use the Sign Up tab to create an organization account. That administrator should then add users to their organization's account within the CAST platform. This is done by clicking on the gear in the left menu and clicking Add a User.

    We suggest to create a different organization account for each separate location you have, but it's based on your preference. You could either have multiple locations communicating with each other within, or one global account to communicate across locations.

    Settings Button


    Add a User Button


  • Installing Authenticator
    In order to begin using 2-step authentication, please follow the installation steps below:
    1. Download Authenticator to Your Phone

    Find and download "Authenticator" from the app store.
    Follow in-app instructions for set-up.

    2. Add FusiformCAST in the Authenticator app

    Press "+" on the top right of the screen >> Scan barcode / Manual entry.

    3. Enter code generated by the app

    You're done!



  • Logging In
    1. You'll enter your password

    Whenever you sign in to Fusiform, you'll enter your password as usual. Then, you'll be asked for something else.

    2. A unique code will be generated on the Authenticator application.

    Type in the code generated for "FusiformCAST".
