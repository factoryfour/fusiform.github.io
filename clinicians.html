CLINICIANS

CAST Overview

CAST is a platform that connects clinicians and vendors to make O&P business more efficient by digitizing ordering and communication.
Organization at Ease

Have all your patients digitally accessible, searchable, and manageable from anywhere with our cloud-based system.

Streamlined Orders

Welcome to your new ordering experience. Our digital order forms let you autofill with your own custom templates. They make sure that you have the most updated form and put in all the required information correctly, minimizing mistakes. Enjoy consistency in creating your order forms, whether you're at HQ or on the move.

Instant Communication

Directly message in between clinics and vendors, resolving issues efficiently without enormous amounts of time wasted on phone tag, form inconsistencies, and costly mistakes.

Automated Order Management

Vendors and clinicians remain on the same page with automated notifications about any change of order status, patient updates, and comments. World-class customer service is now easily available to the O&P industry.

3D-Scanning + Annotations

With native 3D scan support, upload and annotate 3D scans of your patients directly in our application. Scanning works, and we give you the tools to love it.

HIPAA-compliant Data Storage

No limits. Attach any file type, as many as you need, and it will be accessible for as long as you'd like. Rest assured that your data is compliant and secure from breaches.
