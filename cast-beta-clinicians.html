Spend less time casting and more time with your patients.
Get Started

Give Casting a Break.

The casting process is wasteful and time-consuming. We're asking you to go completely digital with your custom fabrication.

1.
Scan your Patient

Using a $500 iPad-mounted scanner, take an accurate scan of your patient in under 5 minutes. There, you're done! Now upload that scan into the CAST application.

2.
Annotate

Every patient is unique. That's why CAST allows you to annotate the 3D scan to tell your custom fabricators exactly what changes you need. Any minute details? Don't worry you can process your order forms right within CAST.

3.
Collaborate

We know it can be frustrating if the device you ordered isn't perfect. So why not talk to your custom fab during the process? Collaborate on the same 3D model through annotations and live chat, and give the final OK.
